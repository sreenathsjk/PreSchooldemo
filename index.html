Our Story

A School Built on
Love & Dreams

Baby Star Coaching Center was born from a simple but powerful dream — to give every little child in Garladinne a beautiful beginning. Nestled near APG Bank, opposite Srinivasa Ayyangar Bakery, our school has been a second home for hundreds of children.

We believe the early years of a child's life are magical. Our experienced, caring teachers guide each child through their first steps in reading, writing, and exploring the world — all while ensuring they feel safe, loved, and joyful every single day.

  • Child-centered, play-based approach to early education
  • Warm, nurturing teachers who treat every child as their own
  • Rooted in Indian culture and community values
  • Affordable quality education for all families
  • Transparent communication with parents every step of the way

Our Programs

Two Programs, One Great Start 🎓

Thoughtfully designed programs that prepare your child for school and life with confidence and joy.

🌱

LKG Program

Ages 3–4 Years

Lower Kindergarten is where the magic begins! Children are introduced to a structured yet fun environment that fosters curiosity, social skills, and early literacy. Every day is an exciting discovery through play and creative activities.

🔤 Alphabet Recognition 🔢 Number Concepts 🎨 Creative Arts 🗣️ Oral Language 🤝 Social Skills
🚀

UKG Program

Ages 5–6 Years

Upper Kindergarten builds on a solid foundation, preparing children for formal schooling with advanced literacy, numeracy, problem-solving and emotional intelligence — while keeping learning joyful and engaging.

📖 Reading & Writing ➕ Basic Arithmetic 🧠 Critical Thinking 🌍 Environmental Study 🎭 Creative Expression

Daily Schedule

A Perfectly Balanced Day ⏰

Every hour is crafted to balance learning, play, rest, and creativity — keeping children energized and happy all day long!

8:30 – 9:00

🌅 Morning Welcome & Assembly

Children arrive, freshen up, and gather for morning prayers, pledge, and a fun warm-up song circle.

9:00 – 9:30

🔤 Language & Phonics Time

Interactive alphabet learning, phonics practice, and vocabulary building through songs and flashcards.

9:30 – 10:00

🔢 Numbers & Math Fun

Counting games, shape recognition, pattern activities, and hands-on number exercises.

10:00 – 10:30

🍎 Snack Break & Hygiene

Healthy snack time with lessons in handwashing, table manners, and social interaction.

10:30 – 11:00

🎨 Arts, Crafts & Creative Time

Drawing, coloring, painting, clay work, or seasonal craft projects to express creativity freely.

11:00 – 11:30

🏃 Outdoor Play & Physical Activities

Supervised outdoor games, sports, and free play in the fresh air for physical development.

11:30 – 12:00

📖 Storytelling & Show & Tell

Teachers narrate engaging stories; children share their thoughts building communication skills.

12:00 – 12:30

🎵 Music, Dance & Drama

Rhymes, action songs, simple dance, and role play activities for joyful self-expression.

12:30 – 1:00

🌙 Wind Down & Dismissal

Quiet time, recap of the day's learnings, sharing homework, and a happy send-off home!
