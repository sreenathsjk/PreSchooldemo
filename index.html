About Our School

Where Excellence Meets Education

Sri Chaithanya English Medium School, Garladinne is a premier institution committed to holistic education from Nursery through 10th Class. Located in the heart of Garladinne, Anantapur District, we blend academic rigor with character development to prepare students for a bright future.

Our school follows an English medium curriculum, ensuring students are equipped with strong language skills, scientific temper, and a love for lifelong learning.

🎓
Experienced & Dedicated Faculty

35+ qualified teachers with deep subject expertise and passion for nurturing young minds across all grade levels.

🌐
English Medium Curriculum

Complete English medium instruction from Nursery to 10th, preparing students for competitive exams and higher education.

🔬
Modern Infrastructure

Smart classrooms, science labs, computer labs, library, and sports grounds for all-round development.

Admissions 2025–26

Join the Sri Chaithanya Family

We welcome students of all backgrounds. Our admission process is simple, transparent, and guided by a student-first philosophy.

1
Contact the School

Call us or visit the school between 9 AM – 5 PM (Mon–Sat) to express interest and learn about available seats for your child's class.

2
School Visit & Interaction

Tour our campus, meet the teachers, and let your child interact with our faculty. This helps both families and school assess mutual suitability.

3
Submit Documents

Provide Birth Certificate, previous year's Marks Card/TC, Aadhar card, and 4 passport photos for admission registration.

4
Fee Payment & Enrollment

Complete fee payment and receive your Welcome Kit. Your child is officially a Sri Chaithanya student — the journey begins!
